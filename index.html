Our Mission

Helping each other can
make world better

At Merriment meals our mission is simple to fight hunger and reduce food waste by connecting surplus food from parties, events, and restaurants to those in need. We believe that no one should go hungry when there is an abundance of food available.

At Merriment Meals, we provide nutritious meals, reduce food waste, and strengthen community bonds.

OUR INSPIRATION

Feeding Hope A Food
Donation Initiative

The idea for Merriment meals came from a simple yet powerful observation. After attending a large event where an excessive amount of food was left over, our founder wondered why this perfectly good food should go to waste when so many people struggle to find their next meal
