About us

OUR MISSION

No one will go hungry in the world

Merriment meals was founded by a group of passionate girls who saw an opportunity to make a difference in their local community and create solution for food waste. It all started one evening after a wedding party. Seeing the large quantities of untouched food being thrown away, Miss Subhranjali felt a deep sense of urgency to address this issue and decided to take action. The next day, a call was made to local shelters and food banks, and the first delivery of surplus food was made. The joy and gratitude on the faces of the recipients were unforgettable, and that moment became the driving force behind Merriment meals.

At Merriment meals our mission is simple to fight hunger and reduce food waste by connecting surplus food from parties, events, and restaurants to those in need. We believe that no one should go hungry when there is an abundance of food available.
